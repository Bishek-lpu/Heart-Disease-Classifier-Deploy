The Problem

Cardiovascular diseases are the leading cause of death globally. Early detection is critical, but analyzing complex clinical parameters and interpreting ECG strips is time-consuming and prone to human error. Accessibility to rapid, preliminary screening is severely limited.

Our Solution

We leverage a multi-modal AI approach to provide instant decision support. By combining a Scikit-Learn classifier for tabular clinical data, a TensorFlow CNN for raw ECG strip imagery, and LLM Model for generating patient-friendly summaries, we bridge the gap between raw medical data and actionable insights.

Core Capabilities

Clinical Tabular Analysis

Input 13 distinct vital signs and clinical parameters (like age, cholesterol, and resting BP) to receive an instant probabilistic risk score.

Computer Vision on ECG

Upload a raw 1D/2D ECG image strip. Our custom-trained CNN automatically classifies the rhythm pattern with high confidence intervals.

Generative AI Summaries

Powered by OpenAI, the assistant generates plain-English, non-diagnostic explanations of the AI outputs so patients can better understand their health.
